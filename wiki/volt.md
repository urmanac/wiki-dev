# Volt

Technically speaking, Volt implements LN BOLTs 2, 3, 5, 10, and 11.  Open and operate payment channels with other Urbit ships, manage liquidity, and much more. We use LND to bridge payments outside the Urbit network. 

A grant proposal for this project was accepted by the Urbit Foundation, which you can read here: [urbit.org/grants/lightning-development](https://urbit.org/grants/lightning-development) <br>
If you have an idea, want to learn as an apprentice or fancy getting paid as a developer, please, have a look over the Urbit Foundation's grants program here: [urbit.org/grants](https://urbit.org/grants)
