# Orca

A chat channel federation tool for <a class="wlink">%graph-store</a> based groups. <sup>[1]</sup>

<span class="xlink">Orca</span> lets multiple resources share posts. The practical application is that you could have a federated chat amongst many separate groups (each with their own private chats as well, should they like) where all posts display on all joined resources. <sup>[2]</sup>
